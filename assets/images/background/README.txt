HERO BACKGROUND IMAGE
=====================

Place your hero background image here:

Location: /assets/images/background/hero-bg.jpg

RECOMMENDED SPECS:
- Format: JPG (compressed)
- Dimensions: 1920x1080px or larger
- File size: Under 500KB for performance
- Style: Professional photo of yourself or abstract/creative background

The image will have:
- A gradient overlay (dark to light from left to right)
- Slight brightness/contrast adjustments for premium look
- Positioned center-right to keep left side darker for text

If no image is found, the template will use a fallback background.

To replace:
Simply add your image as "hero-bg.jpg" in this folder.
